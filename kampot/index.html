Over flow swimming pool

Onederz Kampot is the best place for pool lovers !

This over flow pool is very big and surrounded by beautiful garden.

We have many sun beds for you and there is nothing block sunshine all day !

Lounge

Our spacious ground floor lounge features a beautiful garden terrace and a great restaurant. It’s the perfect spot to meet fellow travelers, enjoy meals, read, or unwind with various facilities catering to your needs.

Tour and travel desk

Our tour desk offers essential information and arrangements for tuk-tuk tours to local attractions. We also provide bus tickets to various destinations like Sihanoukville and Phnom Penh, with many companies offering pick-up services at our hostel.

Restaurant

You will enjoy tasty foods and drinks at our restaurant/bar with reasonable price !

Our restaurant/bar is conveniently located next to swimming pool. Beautiful pool and garden view will make your food and drink taste even better !

Shared bathroom (for dorm rooms)

Every floor (our dorm rooms are on the Ground, 1st, 2nd and 3rd floor) has a lady bathroom and a man bathroom. Every bathroom has 2 shower rooms with electric shower system, 2 toilet rooms and 2 lavatories.

Body soap, shampoo and hair dryers are available for free. We also provide towel for free with 5 usd deposit.

Luandry service

Our laundry service is available at $1.50 per kg, with a turnaround time of 24 hours. Just bring your clothes to reception to get started!

Dorms - Wide variety of dormitory rooms

We provide comfortable sleeping space to everyone. Our bunk beds are like boxes that can make you feel having some privacy. You will find electric socket, reading lamp and accessory case in your sleeping space. Although we are hostel, our mattresses, blankets and pillows are as good as items in a hotel. All of our rooms are equipped with well maintained air-conditioner. ...
We placed shared bathrooms outside the dorm room so your sleep will not be disturbed by noise from bathrooms.
Room Locker : We provide large lockers measuring 40cm*50cm*80cm. We also provide padlocks for those who don't have their own. So, you will get 2 keys when you check in: one for the room door and another for the locker.

Private Rooms - Our modern rooms meet your basic needs

Our private rooms have air conditioner, TV, electronic digital security box, electric kettle (with coffee and tea set), hair dryer, 2 bottles of water, free Wifi, towels and etc...

We have a private bathroom inside the room. We have partition wall and curtain between shower booth and toilet.

Experience in Phnom Penh

Killing Field & S21 Museum

Every day

If you are interested in Cambodia's recent history, you cannot miss these places. Tuol Sleng is located within the city (4 km from the hostel), while Killing Fields is on the outskirts of the city (13 km from the hostel) and takes about 40 minutes by tuk-tuk.

Cooking Class

Every Friday

Join us for an exciting amok cooking class! Discover the secrets of this delicious Cambodian dish, learn new techniques, and enjoy a tasty meal. Perfect for all skill levels. Can't wait to cook with you!
